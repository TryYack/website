Team back team collaboration

Yack makes working on a team fun again. Here are some things Yack can help your team with.

Private & public channels

Yack messaging can be public or private when you need to talk to another team member. Channels can also be one-way (broadcast) or private to a group of team members.

Archiving, muting & starring

When you need to focus on only a few projects, Yack lets you mute, archive or star channels to keep them nice and organised.

Granular permissions

Grant permission to only a few people (or many). Permissions can be set on channels & also seperately on teams.

Do not disturb, presences & notifications

When you need to focus you can turn off notifications for a period - or set your presence to busy.

Made for remote work

Yack will let you know what timezone your fellow member is on, in private conversations.

Unlimited history

We won't limit the number of searchable members for the alpha version. Keep updated about premium plans by subscribing to our newsletter.

Appstore & platform

Build your own apps, private apps or install other people's apps from the app store.

Polls App

Get feedback from your channel members by posting polls the channel - there is no limit to how many (or few) polls are allowed per team.

Google Drive support App

Attach Google Drive accounts so you can access your files quickly & easily.

FAQ

This seems very new, what's the story?

You're correct! Yack is a new messaging app built from the ground up to make communication & collaboration easier for your team.

But, is this just another Slack or Microsoft Teams?

You're not wrong, but we believe there are a few things that Yack does a lot better. Being mostly open source & our app platform to name a few.

Where is the pricing & plans info?

We are busy finalising our paid plans - please subscribe to our newsletter to keep updated here and how premium plans will affect current features.

Data & security is pretty important to us.

We're rolling out GDPR compliance very very soon and will update you as soon as we can about that - subsribe to our newsletter to keep updated!

Why not open source the API as well?

By playing a few things closer to the chest, we feel more confident about giving users the best experience.

When will a more public release happen?

Soon - subscribe to the newsletter to keep updated!

I need to connect my GitHub (or other) account

We are busy building more integration, but in the meantime you'll be able to use the Zapier app really.

Something has broken / I can't log in.

Oh no, sorry about that. Unfortunately Yack is still in alpha, so please expect things to break. You can email us at support@yack.co for more info.
